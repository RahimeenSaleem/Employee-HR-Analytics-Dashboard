# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce data and provide actionable HR insights. The dashboard helps HR professionals and business leaders monitor employee demographics, salary distribution, workforce composition, employee tenure, and future workforce trends.

The report combines descriptive analytics with forecasting to support strategic workforce planning and data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Human Resources teams require centralized reporting to better understand their workforce and answer key business questions such as:

- Which departments have the largest workforce?
- What is the gender distribution among employees?
- Which departments have the highest salary costs?
- How does employee tenure vary across departments?
- What are the expected future salary and workforce trends?

This dashboard provides interactive visualizations that help answer these questions efficiently.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Employee Dashboard

Provides an overview of the organization's workforce.

### Features

- Total Employees KPI
- Employees by Department
- Gender Distribution
- Salary Distribution
- Employee List
- Average Tenure by Department

### Business Insights

- Compare workforce size across departments.
- Analyze gender distribution.
- Identify salary distribution patterns.
- Monitor employee tenure across the organization.

---

## 2️⃣ Business Questions

Focuses on answering common HR business questions using interactive visualizations.

### Business Questions Addressed

- Which department has the highest salary cost?
- Which gender earns more on average?
- What is the distribution of employee types?
- Which work location has the largest workforce?
- What is the average employee tenure?

### Features

- Employee Type Distribution
- Average Salary by Gender
- Workforce by Location
- Department Salary Comparison
- Department Filter

---

## 3️⃣ Employee Attrition Report

Analyzes workforce tenure and employee characteristics.

### Features

- Employee Count by Tenure Category
- Average Salary by Tenure
- Department Filters
- Gender Filters
- Employee Type Analysis

### Business Insights

- Understand workforce experience levels.
- Compare salary across tenure groups.
- Explore workforce characteristics using interactive filters.

---

## 4️⃣ HR Forecasting

Forecasts future workforce trends using Power BI forecasting.

### Features

- Salary Forecast
- Employee Growth Forecast
- Confidence Interval Visualization

### Business Insights

- Forecast future salary trends.
- Estimate future workforce growth.
- Support long-term HR planning and budgeting.
